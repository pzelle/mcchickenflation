About McChickflation

McChickflation tracks the standalone price of the McChicken over time so we can visualize what inflation actually feels like at the drive-thru. Each year captures the minimum and maximum price points we can find, with sources and notes so you can explore the underlying context.

McChickflation is a living dataset — new sources welcome.
